# Arlo Email Signature

How to use the Arlo email signature generator.

## How to use

1. **Open the generator**  
   Open `index.html` in your browser, or use the hosted version (e.g. GitHub Pages).

2. **Fill in your details**
   - **Name** – Your full name (e.g. Jane Doe).
   - **Role** – Your job title (e.g. People Ops).
   - **Phone** – Your phone number. Use **Show in signature** to include or hide it.
   - **Email** – Your email address. Use **Show in signature** to include or hide it.

3. **Pick a style**  
   Choose **Style 1**, **Style 2**, or **Style 3** in the preview.

4. **Copy the signature**  
   Click **Copy signature** under the style you want.

5. **Add it in Gmail**
   - Go to **Settings** (gear icon) → **See all settings**.
   - In **General**, find **Signature** and click **+ Create new**.
   - Paste (Ctrl+V / ⌘V) into the signature box.
   - Set when to use it (e.g. for new emails and replies), then **Save Changes**.
